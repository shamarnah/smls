LOGO INSTRUCTIONS
=================

To add the logos:

1. Place your logo image files in this directory (static/images/)
2. Required files:
   - asu_logo.png (ASU - Applied Science Private University logo)
   - fet_logo.png (FET - Faculty of Engineering & Technology logo)
3. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, or SVG
4. Recommended size: 300px width (height will scale automatically)

The logos will appear:
- Side by side on login pages (login.html and admin_login.html)
- Only ASU logo on dashboard pages (student_dashboard.html and admin_dashboard.html)

If your logo files have different names, update the filenames in the respective template files.
